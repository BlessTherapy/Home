Get to know us

Bless Therapy is a Puerto Rican company established since 2011 dedicated to offering consulting, assessment and therapy services in the areas of: Speech Therapy, Early Intervention, Occupational Therapy, Sensory Therapy, Dysphagia, Psychology and Educational Therapy. These therapies can take place physically in the clinic or through our Online Therapy platform. Bless Therapy mission is to simplify and facilitate the access to health services.

Bless Therapy's commitment to children goes beyond just providing therapies. That's why we wanted to innovate in the way we help them. At Bless Therapy, parents have direct access to child development specialists and are led by our professionals in the treatment process of their child.
